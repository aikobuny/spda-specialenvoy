SOUTHERN PHILIPPINES
DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

The Southern Philippines Development Authority (SPDA) serves as a key governmental agency dedicated to fostering trade and investment opportunities in the southern regions of the Philippines. With a focus on economic growth and sustainable development, SPDA plays a pivotal role in promoting the vast potential of the area to both local and international investors. Through strategic initiatives, partnerships, and incentives, SPDA aims to attract investments, facilitate trade activities, and spur socio-economic progress in the southern Philippines. By leveraging the region's unique strengths, resources, and industries, SPDA contributes to the overall advancement of the Philippines' economy while enhancing the livelihoods of its residents.
